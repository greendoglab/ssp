About Us

Stat Staff Professionals is a nurse owned and operated Professional Staffing Organization, specializing in registered nurses and allied health. We offer per diem, contract, and travel opportunities all across the country, and we are committed to finding you the right placement that fits your needs. If you have a valid RN License/certification, are BLS-prepared, and you are committed to patient care - we have your perfect job!
